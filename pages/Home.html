Welcome to our school

A place where every girl can dream big

Located in the peaceful surroundings of Noor Pur, Payagpur, our school has long been committed to providing accessible, quality education for girls in the Bahraich region.

From well-equipped classrooms and a science laboratory to a library, sports ground and green campus, we strive to give our students the environment they need to learn and flourish — supported by an experienced and caring team of teachers.
